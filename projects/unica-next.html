Unica Next

Interaction design

Sneak preview of the latest design for Unica's intelligent building platform. Version 3.0 makes my dream come true: tools organized by users' tasks and combined in one platform.

Tools

Unica Next is intended for building managers and their Unica consultants. Tools are organized by task groups:

  • Fix - maintenance requests
  • Costs - finance (billing, investments, etc)
  • Sight - sensors, statistics, anomalies
  • Care - sustainability & related reporting required by Dutch law
  • Map - buildings & building management policies
  • Docs - documentation archive
