Foreign Student Guide

Graphic design

Information MAI international students need on arrival. Baked and packed. My first printed matter.

Open in PDF (19.7 Mb)

Format: A5 brochure.

Case: It's amazing to study abroad, but onboarding is stressful. When foreign students arrive university representatives meet them in the airport or on campus. Then they should provide some "what, where and how" information. Representatives should repeat all the same each time. Students may be exhausted after the flight and be not pretty focused. Also may not have internet access when they just arrived.

Challenge: Organize all constant information foreign students need on arrival and pack it into a book.
