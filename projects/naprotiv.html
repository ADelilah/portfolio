A movie on the faces

Photo essay in portraits

How beautiful people are doing something they believe in. Backstage of the “Next door”, a short movie by Alexey Znamensky - movie and deaf theater actor now graduating as a director. First and foremost - in portraits captured without a warning.

The filming was exhausting. We were sleeping for 4-5 hours a day, solving problems on the go, dying from the heat in silence with closed windows and no fans as sync sound was recorded. Half of the cast and crew was speaking sign language, other half was not, with only one person able to translate. I was limping because of a swollen wasp bite.

Topics “Next door” is picturing - xenophobia as well as toxic parenting - wasn't pleasunt.

Nevertheless that week brought light into my life and light only, it gifted me not just with future friends and collaborators, but with the personal gold standard of the atmosphere in a team. And the motivation to learn a new language.

So here is a filming crew volunteer's attempt to stop the time and save forever youth and beauty of actual strangers, she sleeps in the same room and eats from the same plate with. While they are floating in something surreal and at the same time - very real. Humanity, may be.
