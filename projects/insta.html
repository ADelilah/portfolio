Instagram genealogy

Study

Hereby I officially declare that mobilography is a photography genre. Let’s take it serious and discover analog ancestors of our beloved Instagram filters. A part of my final paper in Photography Associate's program, summarized and rewritten informally.

A piece of clay

As a piece of any art is tended to change and grow along it’s creation, the artist does not really know what it will be in the end. In photography this matter goes extreme because of the long arm of coincidence. A genius photo can be taken accidentally. Neither technical knowledge nor fancy gear play the key role in the creation of a visual art object. Only the eye capable to find a gem among dozens in the past and nowadays - thousands of pictures turn a person pressed the button into an artist.

There is always one and only true judge - the audience. And if a photo leaves you speechless, does it really matter if it was made with a piece of clay which is enough to make a simplest camera?

Memento mori

I convince - and believe that other photographers may convince the same - that the unconsciousness basis of the impulse to take a picture is the fear of death. So you grab this mysterious time machine - the camera - and trying to make a beautiful sunset endless, you loved ones - young forever, and your own impressions known and remembered. Funny, but often a photographer is too busy bottling life to actually fully enjoy the moment.

But he or she will save some time later, as a picture worth a thousands words. Photography is the fastest way to tell a story, and also is the most straightforward and honest one. No surprise that it won over the text both in registering our personal chronicle and social actions of sharing it with other human beings.

Insta

There is no need to describe, how do we share our personal chronicle today. And we get more likes for visual content because a picture isn’t just the fastest way to tell a story, it is also a fastest way to ‘read’ the story. And the most successful tool for everyday visual storytelling - Instagram - is used by more than 10% of the world population at least once a month.

The brand itself is the reminder that the picture rush started not with social media era, but at the moment when a camera became affordable. Instagram old logo was referring to Polaroid Land Camera - the first commercial instant camera - which also has 1:1 frame aspect ratio. Unveiled in 1948, Polaroid Land Camera was followed by a whole family of so-called toy cameras - mass market cameras, inexpensive because of cheap lenses and limited manual settings.
